# Administrator Manual

Administrators manage platform operations, users, football synchronization, prediction approval, analyst intelligence review, settings, audit logs, reports, and monitoring.

Daily checklist:

- Review pending predictions.
- Review analyst intelligence.
- Confirm football sync health.
- Check wallet withdrawal requests.
- Review audit logs and monitoring.
- Check daily platform reports.

Administrators must only publish approved intelligence and should never expose internal analyst notes to subscribers.
