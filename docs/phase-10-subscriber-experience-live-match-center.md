# Phase 10: Subscriber Experience & Live Match Center

Phase 10 expands the subscriber workspace using the existing football, prediction, and published FPF Intelligence APIs.

## Subscriber Modules

- Dashboard with welcome summary, live matches, upcoming matches, opportunities, notifications, and subscription status
- Global Fixture Center with team, league, country, and date filtering
- Live Match Center with live score, match clock, team statistics, and AI confidence signals
- Opportunity Center showing approved/published FPF Intelligence
- Match Analysis with form, head-to-head, injuries, suspensions/unavailability notes, league position, AI insight, and suggested markets
- Performance page with win rate, ROI, betting history, favorite leagues, and a monthly chart

## Guardrails

- Reuses existing APIs and football data
- Shows only approved/published intelligence to subscribers
- Adds no payment features
- Adds no FPF Coin
- Adds no social media functionality
