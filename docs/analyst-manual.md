# Analyst Manual

Analysts are internal FPF staff or contracted experts. Analysts are not public tipsters and do not have public subscriber profiles.

Workflow:

- Review assigned matches and leagues.
- Use AI assistance summaries for football context.
- Submit intelligence with market, prediction, confidence, risk, reasoning, statistics, source notes, brief explanation, and recommended stake.
- Submit for admin review.
- Revise only when admin requests changes.

Do not guarantee outcomes or fixed returns.
